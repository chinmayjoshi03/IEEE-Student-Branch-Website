Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become an IEEE member?

You can become an IEEE member by visiting our registration page and filling out the membership form. Student membership is available at discounted rates.

What events do you organize?

We organize technical workshops, seminars, coding competitions, IEEE Day celebrations, and various professional development activities throughout the year.

How can I join a technical chapter?

You can join our technical chapters by contacting us through this form or visiting our chapters page for more information about specific societies.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes, we collaborate with industry partners to provide internship and project opportunities for our members. Check our activities page for current openings.
