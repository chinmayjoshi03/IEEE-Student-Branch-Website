About IEEE Student Branch CEV

The IEEE Student Branch at College of Engineering Vadakara has been a cornerstone of technical education and professional development since 2011.

The IEEE Student Branch at College of Engineering Vadakara has been a cornerstone of technical education and professional development since 2011. We are committed to advancing technology for humanity through student engagement and innovation.

Our organization encompasses multiple technical societies and affinity groups, providing students with diverse opportunities to explore their interests, develop technical skills, and build lasting professional networks.
